Quick Answers

Common Questions

How do I report a faulty charger? +
Call 01327 857 271 or use the contact form on this page. Please note the zone and bay number shown on the unit. Engineers are on site on all event days.
Can I pre-book an EV bay? +
Pre-booking is available for Zone Delta ultra-rapid chargers on race days. All other bays are first-come, first-served. Create an account to use the booking system.
How do I request accessibility assistance? +
Use the form on this page, selecting "Accessibility" as the subject. You can also call the accessibility helpline on 01327 857 200.

Getting Here

How to Reach Us

  • By Road
    • From the M1: Exit at Junction 15A, follow A43 south towards Towcester
    • From the M40: Exit at Junction 11, follow A422 and then A43
    • Postcode for sat-nav: NN12 8TN
  • By Public Transport
    • Train to Northampton or Milton Keynes Central
    • Event-day shuttle buses depart from both stations
  • On Arrival
    • Follow EV and accessible parking signage from the main gate
    • Stewards are present at Gates 1, 2, and 5 on all event days
