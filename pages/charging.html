Connector Guide

Which Connector Do I Need?

  • AC Charging (Slow & Standard)
    • Type 2 (Mennekes) — universal connector for all modern EVs in Europe, including Tesla
    • 3-pin plug — domestic socket; not recommended for regular use
  • DC Fast Charging
    • CCS (Combined Charging System) — standard for most European EVs; supports up to 150 kW
    • CHAdeMO — older standard used by some Nissan and Mitsubishi models
  • Tesla Vehicles
    • Use Type 2 AC chargers natively — no adapter needed
    • CCS adapters are available on loan from the on-site information kiosk
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pre-book a charging bay? +
Pre-booking is only required for Ultra-Rapid chargers in Zone Delta on race days. All other chargers operate on a first-come, first-served basis.
What payment methods are accepted? +
All chargers accept contactless card payment. RFID charging cards (Osprey network) are also accepted and give a small per-kWh discount.
Is charging included in the parking fee? +
No. Charging is billed separately on a per-kWh basis. Parking fees (where applicable) are handled at the gate kiosk.
What do I do if a charger is faulty? +
Call our support line on 01327 857 271 or report it via the Contact page. A qualified engineer is on site on all event days and most weekdays.
Can I use a Tesla vehicle at these chargers? +
Yes. Tesla vehicles can use CCS chargers with a CCS adapter, which is available from the on-site information kiosk as a loan item. Type 2 AC chargers are natively compatible with Tesla.
