Our Story

SMART PARKING
FOR THE FUTURE

Silverstone Smart Parking was developed to meet the growing demand for EV infrastructure at one of the world's most iconic motorsport venues. As Formula 1 and the wider industry transition towards electric and sustainable technology, we're ensuring the visitor experience matches that ambition.

Our platform gives every driver — arriving by EV, bike, or bus — real-time information about parking availability, charger status, and accessible routes, all before they leave home.

Technology

How It Works

Silverstone Smart Parking uses IoT sensors embedded in each parking bay to detect occupancy in real time. This data is pushed to our platform via a low-power wireless network and displayed live on this website.

  • Bay Sensors
    • Magnetic field sensors detect vehicle presence
    • Data updated every 30 seconds
    • Battery-powered — 5-year lifespan per unit
  • Charger Monitoring
    • OCPP 2.0 protocol for all networked chargers
    • Real-time fault detection and alert system
  • Platform
    • Built with HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript
    • Hosted on Netlify with automatic SSL
    • WCAG 2.2 AA compliant throughout
