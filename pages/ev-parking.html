📋 EV Bay Rules

  • Eligibility
    • EV bays are for plug-in electric and hybrid vehicles only
    • Petrol or diesel vehicles will be issued a penalty notice
  • Charging Etiquette
    • Move your vehicle once charging is complete
    • Do not unplug another vehicle's connector
    • Report faulty units to the helpline: 01327 857 271
  • Safety
    • Keep charging cables tidy — do not leave across walkways
    • In an emergency, press the red stop button on the charger unit
