TRAVEL & ACCESSIBILITY

Accessible routes, sustainable travel options, and visitor assistance for everyone at Silverstone.

Image: Unsplash — free to use under Unsplash Licence

♿
Accessible Routes
  • Drop-off Points
    • Level-access drop-off at all main entrances
    • Designated accessible drop-off at Gates 1 and 2
  • Parking
    • 22 accessible bays across all zones
    • Blue Badge required — verify at steward post on arrival
  • Pathways
    • Hardstanding paths throughout all car parks
    • Steward assistance available at Gates 1, 2, and 5
    • Hearing loop at all information kiosks
⬇ Download Accessible Route Map (PDF)
🚌
Sustainable Travel
  • Shuttle Bus Service
    • Runs from Northampton Station on all event days
    • Accessible coaches with ramp boarding
    • Free for Blue Badge holders
  • Car Sharing
    • Designated drop-off zones at all perimeter gates
    • Liftshare partnership — find matches at liftshare.com
  • Zero-Emission Travel
    • Electric shuttle buggies run circuits within grounds
    • Carbon offset option when booking parking online
🚲
Cycling to Silverstone
  • Routes
    • Signed cycling routes from Towcester (5 miles, largely flat)
    • NCN Route 70 passes within 1 mile of the circuit
  • Facilities on Arrival
    • 42 secure bike racks at Zones A and B
    • Covered racks and lockers near Gate 3
    • E-bike charging — first hour free
🗺 View Bike Charging Stations
📞
Visitor Assistance
  • On-Site Support
    • Stewards at all car parks from 2 hours before events
    • Medical points marked on all maps and apps
  • Sensory Support
    • Quiet zones available — ask at any steward post
    • Ear defenders and maps available at the welcome desk
  • Contact
    • Accessibility helpline: 01327 857 200
    • Email: access@silverstone.co.uk
