BIKE & E-BIKE CHARGING

Secure bike parking and dedicated e-bike charging racks across Silverstone Circuit. Sustainable travel starts here.

Image: Unsplash — free to use under Unsplash Licence

Charging Stations

Bike Charging Locations

Zone A — Main Entrance

16 racks with e-bike charging. Covered shelter. Near Gate 2. Bosch and Shimano connectors available.

Open
Zone B — South Car Park

12 racks adjacent to the shuttle bus stop. Open-air but sheltered. Universal connector panel.

Open
Gate 3 — Covered Lockers

Secure individual lockers with built-in charging. First hour free. Suitable for high-value or cargo e-bikes.

Limited

🌿 Why Cycle to Silverstone?

  • Environmental Benefits
    • Zero emissions per journey

  • Practical Advantages
    • Skip road queues on race days
    • Dedicated secure parking — no waiting
    • Free e-bike charging at all stations
  • Route Support
    • Signed cycling routes from Towcester (5 miles)
    • NCN Route 70 passes within 1 mile of the circuit
