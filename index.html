Why Silverstone Smart Parking

Designed for the Modern Visitor

As Formula 1 and motorsport embrace sustainable technology, Silverstone is leading the way with world-class EV infrastructure. Our smart parking platform gives every visitor — whether arriving by car, bike, or bus — the information they need to arrive confidently.

  • Electric Vehicle Support
    • 320 dedicated EV-only bays across 4 zones
    • 150kW ultra-rapid chargers at Zone Alpha
    • Compatible with all major EV connectors
  • Sustainable Travel Options
    • 42 bike and e-bike charging stations
    • Shuttle buses from Northampton Station on event days
    • Carbon offset available when booking online
  • Accessible for Everyone
    • Dedicated accessible bays near all main entrances
    • Steward assistance at Gates 1, 2, and 5
    • Hearing loops at all information kiosks
